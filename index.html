Moana

Moana,a brave Polynesian girl,sails across the ocean to restore the heart of Te Fiti and save her island.Through her journey, she discovers her true destiny as a leader and protector.

Snow White

Snow White escapes her evil stepmother, befriending seven dwarfs. She is awakened from a poisoned sleep by a prince's kiss.

Mulan

Mulan disguises herself as a man to take her father's place in the Chinese army. Through bravery and ingenuity, she saves China and earns respect as a true hero.
